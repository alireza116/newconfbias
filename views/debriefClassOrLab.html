What you should know about this study:
The purpose of this study is to understand to what degree individuals update their beliefs about relationships between two variables in light of new data. For each set of variables you were asked to draw your belief about the most likely relationship before and after viewing data about that relationship using different types of data visualizations. Your responses will help us better understand how data visualization affects peoples' judgments about different phenomena.

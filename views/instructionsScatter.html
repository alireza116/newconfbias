In this study you will also encounter charts that represent real data about the relationship between two variables. These scatter charts show a number of observations. Each circle in the scatter plot shows the values for the two variables that were measured for one observation. For example, the top right circle in the following chart represents a person who has a very high height and weight. The circle at the bottom left represents a person who has a very low height and very low weight.

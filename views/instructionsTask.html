Study Instructions

In this study you will make judgments based on your beliefs about the relationship between pairs of variables. For each set of variables (e.g., X = height, Y = weight) :

  1. you will be asked about how you think those variables are related in the real world.
  2. you will be shown a sample of data collected from a larger population that includes measurements of the two variables.
  3. you will be again be asked how you think the two variables are related after taking into account the data you are shown.

There will be two rounds in the study which differ in how the data for each set of variables is presented to you. Throughout the task, please respond to the best of your ability based on how you think variables are related to each other.
